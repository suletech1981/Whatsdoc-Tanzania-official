Our Story & Vision

SuleTech Tanzania is a tech-powered social initiative founded by William Samson Sule in 2025. It started as WhatsDoc Tanzania, a simple document typing solution, but grew into a fully-fledged digital powerhouse.

Our goals include:

  • Making document services available to teachers, students & institutions easily
  • Providing quality and creative graphic design for branding across Tanzania
  • Innovating education through smart tools, templates, and web services
  • Empowering youth with online income through digital skills

We serve clients across Tanzania through WhatsApp, website orders, and remote collaboration.
